What We Offer

ELBRIDGE

Elbridge is an Enterprise Solution deployed through licensed institutions like banks, Superagents, and PTSPs to SMEs and Agent Aggregators to have control of their transactions acquired via POS terminals and other channels. This is Agency Banking 2.0 … The future of Financial Inclusion and payment aggregation at scale. The platform gives the client access to profile agents/merchants, assign pricing plans, and manage settlements. Elbridge as a system has various units including TMS, User Management System, Middleware, and Android Mobile Application. All stakeholders are winners with Elbridge.

elPAY

elPAY aggregates transactions acquired via the POS by agents of licensed Superagents and banks.
